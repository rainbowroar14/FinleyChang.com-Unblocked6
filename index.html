Physics Sandbox

Controls stay here while you play. Click the dark overlay center to capture the mouse. Press H anytime to hide or show this panel.

Start

Click center screen

Locks the pointer so you look with the mouse. Press Esc to release the cursor.

Move & camera

W A S D

Walk forward, left, back, and right relative to where you are looking.

Space

Jump when you are on the ground. In ghost mode it moves you up instead.

Ctrl (left)

While ghost mode is on, fly downward.

Ghost uses no collision so you can pass through objects and the floor.

Shift hold

Toggle ghost (no-clip) flight while held. Faster than walking.

G

Cycles physics time only: normal → 20% speed → frozen → normal. Your movement, camera, and grab target stay at full speed; only the physics simulation (objects, ropes, rockets, balloons) scales.

Spawn menu

Q hold

Opens the catalog. Choose Shapes, Props, Presets, or Debug tools. Release Q to close and return to the game.

E

Spawns the selected item at the surface you are aiming at (crosshair). If you aim at empty sky it spawns ahead of you above the ground. Under Props, Balloon (floats) is a tiny light balloon that drifts and climbs on its own.

Grab & throw

Mouse left hold

Physics grab: pulls the object toward your crosshair on a short rod. Objects are kept from being dragged through the floor.

Mouse right

While holding something with a normal grab, throws it in the look direction.

Debug mode

0

Switches between normal physics grab and debug tools. Status pill turns yellow in debug.

Q → Debug tab

Move — click-drag to reposition without the springy constraint.
Resize — click an object, then scroll the mouse wheel (single box or sphere bodies only).
Delete — click an object to remove it.
Spawn here — click a surface to spawn the item you last picked in Shapes/Props/Presets.
Balloon gun — each click adds lift equal to the default green sphere’s mass (same “weight,” reversed as buoyancy). A separate balloon mesh rises on a pink string from the hit point; when the string goes taut it tugs the object upward. BALLOON_POWER in the script scales total lift. When lift × power > mass, the object rises; the extra drives upward acceleration × gravity strength.
Rope gun — inelastic max length: slack gently draws the ends together; past max length it resists stretch with capped impulses (stable).
Cord gun — spring toward the length when you fired: pulls when stretched, pushes when compressed, with damping and a safe impulse cap so energy does not blow up.

Switching debug tools or pressing 0 cancels an unfinished rope or cord shot.

Rocket & launch pad

F

Looks for a rocket under the crosshair and toggles its engine. Thrust pushes along the rocket’s nose until you press F again on the same rocket. Strength uses ROCKET_THRUST_POWER in the script.

Launch pad

Pink pad: bounces and adds an upward + forward kick when something touches it.

Physics tuning

In the script: BODY_DENSITY scales how heavy objects are from their size, BALLOON_POWER scales balloon lift, ROCKET_THRUST_POWER scales rocket thrust. Rope/cord: ROPE_MAX_STIFFNESS, ROPE_MAX_DAMPING, ROPE_SLACK_PULL_K, ROPE_IMPULSE_CAP, CORD_SPRING_K, CORD_SPRING_C, CORD_IMPULSE_CAP. Buoyancy uses the same gravity strength as the physics world.

Tips

Aim at the floor or props to spawn on top of them. Wedge is a solid ramp; use launch pads under balls or crates for big hops. Props include an NPC that wanders the floor.
